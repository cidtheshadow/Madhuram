Why choose us?

Madhuram’26 is SLIET Longowal’s premier social and cultural festival, a
two-day extravaganza celebrating diversity, creativity and unity. It brings
together students from various backgrounds, cultures, and talents to
showcase their skills, exchange ideas and foster a sense of belonging. With
over 10,000 attendees from across the country, Madhuram offers a valuable
opportunity for brands to gain visibility and leave a lasting impression. In
return for sponsorship, we guarantee well-deserved recognition through
prominent placement across SLIET, featuring your brand on the
Madhuram’26 official website, banners, performance stages, and all social
media posts. We offer one title sponsorship along with eight distinct
sponsorship tiers tailored to meet specific promotional needs. For more
information, visit us at madhuramstiet.com.

Our Mission

At Madhuram'26, our mission is to create a vibrant and inclusive cultural
fest that celebrates diversity, fosters creativity, and brings people together.
Through this celebration, we aim to foster mutual respect, understanding
and appreciation among individuals from diverse backgrounds.

Above all, our mission is to build bridges that connect hearts and minds. We
believe that the power of cultural expression can create lasting bonds and
unforgettable memories. By fostering an environment of inclusivity and
open-mindedness, we aim to inspire connections that transcend borders,
leaving a legacy of unity, harmony, and shared experiences for all who join
us on this extraordinary cultural journey.
